ABOUT US

CrimeEye is a crime tracking web app designed to keep the people of San Francisco informed about criminal activity in their area. The app uses cutting-edge technology to monitor crime reports and provide real-time updates to users, helping them stay aware of potential risks in their neighborhoods. As a user of CrimeEye, you can access crime maps, receive alerts, and stay up-to-date on local news and events related to crime in San Francisco. With its user-friendly interface and comprehensive coverage, CrimeEye is the ultimate tool for anyone looking to stay safe and informed in one of America's most vibrant cities.
